Netball Rules

  • No walking or running with ball
  • Nails must be cut short
  • You can only take one step before passing the ball
  • Each position has their own rules that must be followed
  • 3 Second hold with ball
  • 3feet away when you're defending
  • Player cannot make physical contact with one another
  • No earing must be worn during games
  • You must be atleast 3feet away from passing the ball to a player
  • No chewing gum on the courts
  • Centers can run everywhere around the courts except for the circle on the side
